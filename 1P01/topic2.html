Famous People

DARUDE

Most commonly known for his hit song Sandstorm (1999), Darude is a trance artist from Eura, Hinnerjoki, Finland. His real name is Ville Virtanen and he began writing music in 1996 (Chirita, 2015).

KIMI RÄIKKÖNEN

Kimi Räikkönen is a talented Finnish Formula One driver. He has raced for nine seasons in Formula One, winning in 2007. In 2013, he signed a contract with Ferrari for two years. In 2007, he was also the highest paid driver in motor sports (Chirita, 2015).
